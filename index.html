About
the Series
The CLEAR workshop series brings together researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders to address transparency in AI systems through collaborative and participatory approaches. Each edition is co-located with a major AI conference and features presentations, discussions, and community-building around trustworthy and transparent AI.
